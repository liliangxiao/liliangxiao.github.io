Remained Challenges

Although the improved artificial potential field method can effectively avoid the minimum trap, it has poor adaptability in unknown dynamic environment. The reason is that the VPFDCM cannot predict the movement state of obstacles, and for obstacles that move faster, the model can easily cause information loss. LSTM can better handle time series tasks.
